Tillett Hostel and Guesthouse

Lodging ($)

Tillett Hostel is the first and only hostel in the Virgin Islands! We invite you to enjoy the unique atmosphere of Tillett Gardens. Tillett Gardens is a 60+ year old local artists' and business community, started by Jim & Rhoda Tillett in 1959 and is still family owned and operated. The Garden is home to several restaurants hosting Live Music almost every night, art classes, gift shop, Artist's Studios, Live Performance Theater, a salon, and so much more!

Sapphire Village is conveniently located on the east end of St. Thomas, offering some of the best views for owners and guests with quick, easy access to the Sapphire beach, top rated in the Virgin Islands for snorkeling, crystal clear waters and powdery white sand.

Dinghy's Beach Bar and Grill

Restaurant ($)

Beach side tiki bar and cafe located on Honeymoon Beach Water Island; featuring fresh locally caught fish, sandwiches, burgers, wraps, and many other options. Dinghy's also has golf cart, kayak, snorkel, and beach chair rentals with live music on the weekends!

Amalia Café

Restaurant ($$)

The Spanish restaurant Amalia Café, situated in the heart of Charlotte Amalia, St. Thomas has become one of the most dependable dining spots in St. Thomas. The menu is heavy on tapas, with other favorite Spanish entrees. At Amalia Café you enjoy a taste of Spain in the Caribbean.

Restaurant ($$$)

Caribbean Fish Market has an array of seafood from local fishermen and fresh herbs that are grown locally. Complete with a premium crafted cocktail menu and breathtaking view of Cowpet Bay, and be sure to join for Sunday morning brunch, with live music and bottomless mimosas!
